For the first 14 years of my life, I lived in Hudson, New Hampshire, a small-ish town near Nashua. In 8th grade, I ended up moving to Foxboro, Massachusetts, which was my home before coming to Harvard. There, I was an athlete on the wrestling and track teams and an active member in several clubs, including DECA, Debate Club, and others. I ended up graduating as salutatorian of my class.
